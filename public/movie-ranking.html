Best Movues According To Me

Top 3 Best Movies are:

Maleficent

Maleficent, a kind-hearted fairy who changed after her lover Stefan deceived her, places a curse on his daughter Aurora.
But soon she realizes that only Aurora can bring peace to the troubled country.

Entertainment

Akhil, an underdog and a failure, comes to know that his real father is a rich diamond merchant who has just passed away.
When he goes to retrieve his wealth, he learns that a dog has inherited it.

Bumble Bee

During the Cybertron Civil War, Optimus Prime sends Autobot scout B-127 to Earth to form a base where they can regroup.
Later, the scout befriends a girl named Charlie, who names him Bumblebee.
